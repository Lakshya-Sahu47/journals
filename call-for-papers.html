Call for Papers

Lex Mente Law Journal invites original, unpublished manuscripts for its upcoming issue. We welcome contributions from academicians, legal professionals, researchers, and students across diverse areas of law and allied disciplines.

Submissions may address any contemporary legal issue. Interdisciplinary topics with legal significance are also encouraged. Suggested areas include but are not limited to Constitutional Law, Criminal Justice, International Law, Human Rights, and emerging legal paradigms.

Important Dates

Summer Issue

  • Submissions Open: 1st April
  • Last Date for Submission: 31st May
  • Tentative Publication Date: 15th July

Winter Issue

  • Submissions Open: 1st October
  • Last Date for Submission: 30th November
  • Tentative Publication Date: 15th January

Submission Categories

  • Research Papers (3000–6000 words)
  • Case Commentaries (1500–3000 words)
  • Legislative/Policy Notes (1000–2000 words)

Submission Process

Manuscripts must be submitted via email to: submissions@lexmente.in

Please ensure that the file is in Microsoft Word format (.doc/.docx).

Subject Line Format: "Submission — [Category] — Lex Mente [Issue Name]"

Review and Publication Procedure

  • All manuscripts will be evaluated through a double-blind peer review process.
  • Authors will be notified of the editorial decision within 3–4 weeks of submission.

Formatting and Ethical Guidelines

Authors are required to adhere to Lex Mente's formatting standards, citation style, and ethical submission policy. Detailed guidelines can be found on the Submission Guidelines page.

Contact for Queries

For any further information, feel free to write to us at: info@lexmente.in

We look forward to your scholarly contribution to Lex Mente — Where Law Meets Mind.
