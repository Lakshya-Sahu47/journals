About Lex Mente

Lex Mente is a student-led, peer-reviewed legal journal dedicated to advancing rigorous legal research and thought leadership in the discipline of law. Our objective is to provide a dynamic platform for scholars, academicians, practitioners, and students to engage with contemporary legal developments and contribute original academic insights to the evolving legal discourse.

We are committed to academic integrity, editorial independence, and inclusive representation in legal scholarship.

The journal publishes high-quality contributions in the form of research articles, case commentaries, and policy notes, with a focus on clarity, relevance, and critical analysis.

Our Review Process

Every submission received undergoes a double-blind peer review process and is assessed on parameters of originality, analytical depth, and academic value. Lex Mente operates under a Creative Commons Attribution-NonCommercial 4.0 International License, ensuring free access to knowledge while protecting author's rights.

Our Vision

Driven by a diverse and visionary editorial team, Lex Mente aspires to become a trusted name in student-led legal publishing. Alongside our journal issues, we aim to organize legal writing competitions, policy workshops, and academic collaborations to promote research-oriented legal culture in India and beyond.
